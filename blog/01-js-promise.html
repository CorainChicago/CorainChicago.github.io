JavaScript Promise of a New Day

AKA: Always Keep Your Promises

November 18, 2016

JavaScript has a concept of a promise. It is not alone, promises are everywhere. All kittens in the shelter are playful and cute, not all cats are playful and cut. Long story short, consider adopting a cat older than 1 years old and always prepare for the promise to have at least two states. A promise can succeed and can fail. But what is a promise? Ajax is a form of of a promise - a call to an outside service to get or set data that returns either success or failure.
